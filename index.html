Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery? +

Yes, we do! If you place your order before 2:00 PM GMT, we can deliver it the very same day within our local delivery radius.

How long will my flowers last? +

With proper care, our bouquets typically last between 7 and 10 days. We include a packet of flower food and care instructions with every delivery to help you get the most out of your blooms.

Can I request a custom arrangement? +

Absolutely. We love creating bespoke pieces. You can give us a call or visit the shop to discuss your preferred colour palette, flower types, and budget.

Do you do flowers for weddings and events? +

We certainly do. We offer full floral styling for weddings, corporate events, and parties. Please contact us at least 3 months in advance to book a consultation.
